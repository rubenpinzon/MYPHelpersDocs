Arts Command Terms

Taken from the Arts Guide. Hover over any term to see the definition

Command terms
CreateEvolve from one’s own thought or imagination, as a work or an invention. CritiqueProvide a critical review or commentary, especially when dealing with works of art or literature. (See also “Evaluate”.) DemonstrateMake clear by reasoning or evidence, illustrating with examples or practical application.
DevelopImprove incrementally, elaborate or expand in detail. Evolve to a more advanced or effective state. EvaluateMake an appraisal by weighing up the strengths and limitations. (See also “Critique”.) ExploreUndertake a systematic process of discovery.
FormulateExpress precisely and systematically the relevant concept(s) or argument(s). IdentifyProvide an answer from a number of possibilities. Recognize and state briefly a distinguishing fact or feature. OutlineGive a brief account or summary.
PresentOffer for display, observation, examination or consideration. UseApply knowledge or rules to put theory into practice.
